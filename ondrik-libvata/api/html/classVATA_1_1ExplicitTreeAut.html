ExplicitTreeAut VATA::ExplicitTreeAut::CollapseStates ( const StateToStateMap &  collapseMap) const

Collapses states according to the passed map.

This method renames the states occuring in the automaton according to the collapseMap. collapseMap does not need to be injective, i.e. there may be more than one state mapping to the same state, in which case the two states are effectively collapsed into one.

Computes the specified simulation relation on the automaton.

This method computes the simulation relation specified in the params structure among the states of the automaton. The relation is output as a matrix, indexed from 0. The mapping of indices of columns and rows to the states of the automaton is output by the transl translator.

Unites a pair of automata.

Function for the union of two automata. It takes a pair of automata, renames their states and then merges them into a single automaton. States are renamed by the default dictionary or by a user defined dictionary, so they may be overlapping.
